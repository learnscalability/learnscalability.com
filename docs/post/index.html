Motivation

learnscalability.com is a project that presents topics on distributed systems. The focus is on patterns, algorithms and tools that are the building blocks of modern scalable systems. Along with that, there will be reviews of relevant research papers, analyses of existing open-source systems and solutions to design exercises.
